Admission Procedure

ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA FOR ADMISSION IN B.TECH

The Institution offers 2 entry schemes for the students. Eligibility criterion for a candidate who is seeking admission in B Tech. course is: JEC helpdeskJec Admission help.you can apply directly our college campus or Online at forms.jeckukas.org.in .

B.Tech: 1st Year

Admission is purely based on AICTE norms. The Institute recognizes scores of Joint Entrance Examination (JEE-Main) and Rajasthan Engineering Admission Process (REAP). The pre-requisite is minimum 45% marks as aggregate in class XIIin Physics, Mathematics (compulsory) and Chemistry/Computer science/Bio-tech/Biology from any recognized Board or equivalent.

Lateral entry in Second Year (Direct Admission)

Diploma holders can take admission in the second year as per government norms. The Government admission coordination committee will fill up these seats through centralized counseling after receiving applications. 20% seats in addition to the total intake are available in each discipline. The pre-requisite is minimum 45% aggregate marks in the respective branch from any recognized institute.
